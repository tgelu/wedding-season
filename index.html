Vendor Spotlight

Whispering Vintage

Whispering Vintage is the company you need for your event if what you envision is vintage, bohemian or any type of whimsy design. The services the company offers vary depending on your needs. You can either borrow the items you need: peacock chairs, rugs, vintage tableware, you name it or hire Sara who is so sweet and has such a creative soul to style the entire event for you: flowers, props, paper suite and all. Do check her website and Instagram profile to get in touch with Sara for more information.
